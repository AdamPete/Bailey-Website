HopeStems: Breaking the stigma

While attending Purdue University I was approached by the School of Pharmacy to create an advertisement for their upcoming exhibit: The Brainflower, to accompany their #HopeStems mission.

For this project I had full creative control to shoot and edit everything myself; including all cinematography, video effects, and audio.

This spot won an Award of Excellence at the BEA 2020 Film Festival.
